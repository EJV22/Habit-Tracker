# Habit-Tracker


    Outlines when you went to bed in the recent week, your weekly water intake,
what your goals are, and compares it to the previous week's stats.


    Do you ever feel disorganized or seem to struggle with creating an effective schedule?
Maybe you want to be more consistent with your goals and wish to keep track of your progress.
This app/program helps you plan out your day-to-day life and keeps you in the loop of your own
goals and progress. It handles things such as bedtime and weekly reminders or
to-do lists. How does it do that? Well the program asks for what thing you want to keep track of
then stores the information you give it (ex. what time you went to bed), it can then relay back
all the information stored whenever you need it! What if you need to set a list of reminders
throughout the week? Well do not worry, it will also store various tasks that you can access
through the 'to-do' menu. All the progress is stored with an option, and if you want to
start over, it allows you to reset weekly.

It uses an Input file called habit_data.txt
