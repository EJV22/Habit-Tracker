# Habit-Tracker


    Outlines when you went to bed in the recent week, your weekly water intake,
    what your goals are, and compares it to the previous week's stats

**How to use**

After opening the code on your most recent python version, you are met with a menu

<img width="394" height="287" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ab813801-0301-43ea-9483-4a006a19344a" />

Various options are available!

**Hours**

When choosing either 1 or 2, you are prompted to input the amount of hours of sleep/screen-time
in a week per day

<img width="290" height="105" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/37b085c3-571a-4c29-af7f-9a1ad1579ea5" />

The program will not allow you to go beyond the amount of hours in the day (0 to 24)

<img width="315" height="98" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3bb70622-b9a0-4590-9c6c-959570c99df8" />

**To-do List**

When choosing 3, you are able to type in your weekly goals or to-do list!

<img width="332" height="88" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3e884547-bd9f-482f-8594-551e5a2dcaae" />

Choosing 4 will allow you to clear all those tasks!

<img width="192" height="68" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1c7b0da5-3f24-4408-b393-20597d7b9b05" />

**View Everything**

When choosing 5, you can look at all your statistics at once
It shows the average Sleep/Screen-time hours and your weekly to-do list

<img width="328" height="229" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f67cd81e-ef0d-40e8-b9d6-1e1f81f18655" />

**Save/Load Data**

You are able to save your stats for later! It will automatically create a seperate file called habit_data.txt

You can then load your previous data the next time you use this program!
